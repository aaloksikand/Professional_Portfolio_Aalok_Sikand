About Me

Aalok "All Oak" Sikand is a second generation hotelier in a family business with a portfolio of hotels and restaurants in Los Angeles and San Francisco. He is a licensed Attorney, Real Estate Broker, Certified Hospitality Administrator, and holds a Masters in Business Administration. Aalok has been around hotels his entire life, from living in them from age two, to working in them through college, to witnessing his parents' hotel acquisition and operation journey over the decades. He now joins his brother and uses his unique skillset and decades of experience to innovate in the industry. Aalok's mission is to drive hoteliers' bottom-line and protect their assets through technology.
